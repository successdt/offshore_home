Admin panel

Drag and drop mainpage builder

Set your blog mainpage to display any content type you wish:

  • slideshows
  • posts from categories
  • tags
  • portfolios
  • latest or featured post
  • even individual posts or pages

 

Multiple post formats

With Post Formats, the new approach allows Verticality to define a Post Format and then the post format can be selected in the Publish meta box when saving the post.

  • standard – text post
  • gallery – a gallery of images
  • image – a single image
  • video – a single video
  • audio – multiple audio file

 

Multiple variations of layouts

The built-in layout builder lets you can choose the number of columns for your archive pages, open posts via AJAX, choose pagination or “load more”, sort posts by date, comments, ascending or descending, etc. Overall, there are 3 layout variations:

  • grid-view
  • thumb-view
  • list-view

 

Built-in “Like” system

Enable the “Like” feature to let users easily access faved posts and raise their rating. When a certain post reaches the limit of 50 likes it will become featured.
The like limit can be changed from backend while the number of likes for all posts can be randomly generated.

 

Advanced styling

With Verticality you can set different colors or images for your body. Not the least, you can add background images for individual posts or for the whole site. The theme comes with several predefined background patterns and text color.

 

Multiple slide-shows

You can create an unlimited number of slideshows containing any amount of items and assign them for your mainpage.
Slideshows can be composed from different resource types:

  • Uploaded images or extracted featured images from posts
  • Automated slideshows created from Latest or Featured posts and portfolios.
