A lot of reasons to choose Delight

Admin Panel


A powerful admin panel to set colors, slideshows, fonts, styles and everything else. Have a look! Click here, change something and come back to see the result!

Shortcodes


A lot of useful shortcode buttons to customize any page with slideshows, dropcaps, columns, videos, tooltips, contact forms, lists, accordions, tabs, buttons, price tables…

Videos


Delight allows you to embed videos from YouTube, Vimeo etc, or self-hosted videos (FLV). You can use self-hosted videos as a background too: have a look!

 

I just released Pixwall, a slideshow plugin for WordPress with many features, available on CodeCanyon for 16$.
If you want to see a preview of its features, go to the official page or see the plugin in action on Delight: embedded slideshow and fullscreen slideshow

Lots of possibilities


Decide your background: a Google map, a slideshow, a single image, a color, a video. Decide your page: closed, open, wide, narrow, with or without sidebar.

Many plugins inside


Delight has already got inside a powerful Ajax Contact form generator, breadcrumbs, related posts, share section, page navigation, Flickr thumb gallery and much more.

Galleries


A particular attention to the galleries, with many possibilities, filters, jQuery effects, layouts. By the way: the photographs of this preview are courtesy of Massy……..!!! © All rights reserved

…and, of course, much more, have a look!
